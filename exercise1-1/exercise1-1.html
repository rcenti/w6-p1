Exercise 1.1

Your sketch:

Instructions

Create a sketch with a 600px by 600px canvas and save the following text as a constant:
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet

(If you're wondering what that text is about, it's the first phrase of "lorem ipsum", placeholder text that is widely used by designers to plan text layouts. See more here: https://loremipsum.io/)

Display the text so that it is right aligned and located in the bottom right corner of the canvas, as shown in the image below. Choose your own colours. You can also choose to load a different font. You may find that the text wrapping looks a bit different depending on your chosen font. The displayed text should:

  • take up as much of the bottom right quadrant of the canvas as possible without words disappearing off the bottom of the screen;
  • have a margin of about 20 pixels on the right and bottom edges of the text.

When you're done, check your work using check-exercise1-1.html.

Optional extra--make the text change colour when the mouse is in the bottom right quadrant of the canvas.

Expected output:

You will need to make use of the text display functions textAlign() and textSize(). You will also need to set the width and height of the text box in the text() function.

If you have used all of the above functions and the text still isn't right, check that rectMode() is in the default CORNER mode (i.e. not called).
